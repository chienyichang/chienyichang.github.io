Chien-Yi Chang is an Assistant Professor of Computer Science and Assistant Professor of Law at Durham University. She is affiliated with the Center for Sustainable Development Law and Policy and the JusTN0W initiative. Her research sits at the intersection of artificial intelligence, technology law, and legal theory, with a focus on equitable and sustainable data practices.
